The Studio

Born from architecture.
Forged in technology.

We are architects — trained at the Politecnico di Milano, one of Europe's most rigorous schools of architecture and design. Our foundation is in spatial thinking, material sensitivity, and the discipline of building things that endure.

But we are not traditional real estate agents. We have no interest in listings, negotiations, or closing tactics. What we do have is an obsessive understanding of how buildings are perceived, how spaces are experienced, and how architecture can be communicated to move people.

For over five years, we have been deep in the world of spatial computing — Three.js, WebXR, metaverse platforms, and the spatial web. We write the code that turns floor plans into navigable worlds. And we do it from a single codebase that runs everywhere: your phone, your laptop, a VR headset — one URL, every device.

MERGVS exists at the intersection of these two disciplines. We bring the eye of an architect and the hands of a developer to create immersive property experiences that no template, no plugin, and no generic platform can match.

The name MERGVS itself holds our philosophy. It comes from the Mergus — the diving bird that plunges beneath the surface, past the reflections and the obvious, to find what others cannot see. The word "immersive" shares this same Latin root: mergere, to dive, to submerge. We do the same with every property. We don't decorate the surface — we dive deep into its architecture, its light, its atmosphere, and we extract the hidden story that makes it extraordinary. The meaning is already there, waiting inside every home. MERGVS simply reveals it — immersively, through code and craft.
