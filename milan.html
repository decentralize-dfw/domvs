The Milan Market

Three districts
rewriting the map.

Milan's transformation into Europe's most sought-after residential destination is not accidental. The deliberate reinvention of its inner northern quarter — anchored by fashion, design, and international capital — has produced a property market that rewards those who read its trajectory earliest.

MERGVS brings spatial computing and cinematic production to Milan's prime market. For properties where architectural pedigree and interior design are the primary value drivers, our immersive environments reveal what photography cannot — the spatial experience of living there.

Read our Milan Analysis
Brera
Milan's most prestigious residential address. Cobblestone streets, Liberty-era palazzi, and some of Italy's highest residential price points at €10,000–€15,000/m².
Navigli & Porta Genova
The canal district's creative reinvention has attracted international design community and younger HNWI buyers. Industrial loft conversions and 1900s warehouse residences.
Isola
Milan's fastest-appreciating inner quarter. Anchored by Porta Nuova's architectural landmark status and an influx of international design investment.
Centro Storico & Duomo
Historic centre properties represent Milan's most finite luxury asset class. Piano nobile apartments with original ceiling heights and heritage street presence.
