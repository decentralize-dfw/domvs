What We Deliver

A complete toolkit for
exceptional presentation.

In 2024, 100% of buyers used the internet to search for a home — and 31% cited floor plans as the most valued listing content. Each property is unique. Our services adapt to its story — combining the right tools to create an experience no template could ever match.

Core Package — Included in every engagement

Every MERGVS engagement includes the three core services below. These are non-negotiable — they are what make an experience, not a listing.

3D Interactive Experiences

Fully navigable Three.js virtual tours with spatial audio, room-by-room exploration, and cinematic camera paths. Your buyer walks through the home from anywhere in the world.

Photography Editing

Professional retouching of existing property photographs. Exposure, white balance, framing correction, sky replacement, lens distortion removal — every image calibrated to show the space as the eye actually sees it, not as the camera recorded it.

Cinematic Video Production

From drone sequences to AI-assisted motion design, we transform still moments into cinematic stories. Each video is crafted to evoke the feeling of arrival.

Environment & Context Mapping

Neighbourhood mapping, proximity analysis, lifestyle amenities, transit connections, and view simulations. We sell the life, not just the walls.

Presentation Boards

Digital architectural sheets — plans, sections, elevations, and material palettes — designed with the precision of a studio and the elegance of a gallery.

Virtual Staging & Furnishing

Digital furniture placement, interior design visualization, and style alternatives. Show buyers the potential of every room without moving a single piece.

Optional Add-ons — Tailored to the property

Select any combination below, or let us recommend based on the property type, market, and target buyer profile. For agents: these are included or recommended at no additional charge — no add-on fees, no surprise invoices.

Day & Night Visualization

Time-of-day light simulation, golden hour warmth, twilight drama, and seasonal atmosphere. Show how the home lives across every hour.

Drone & Aerial Perspectives

Bird's-eye context, terrain mapping, property boundaries, and approach sequences. Reveal the scale and setting that ground-level photography cannot.

Floor Plan Digitization

Interactive overlays, precise measurements, room labels, and spatial flow diagrams. Transform static plans into navigable, informative experiences.

Cross-Platform Spatial Web

One codebase, every device. Your property experience runs seamlessly on mobile, desktop, tablet, and VR headsets — a single URL, universally accessible.

Material & Finish Gallery

Close-up textures, material swatches, and finish comparisons presented in an interactive gallery. Buyers feel the quality before they touch it.

Analytics & Engagement Tracking

Buyer heatmaps, dwell time analysis, interaction funnels, and engagement scoring. Know exactly how your listing performs and who is most interested.
