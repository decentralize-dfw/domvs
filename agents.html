The Partnership Agreement

Transparent terms.
No surprises.

We know that clarity matters — especially in a commission-based model. Here is exactly how the partnership agreement works, step by step, with no ambiguity.

Digital-First Process

Our partnership agreement is issued digitally via DocuSign or equivalent within 24 hours of your first enquiry. It is a single-page document outlining the property address, agreed commission rate, and duration terms. No retainer, no exclusivity requirement, no lock-in beyond the active listing period.

  • 01
    You share the listing details

    Send us the property address, type, and approximate price. We assess the property within 24 hours and confirm whether we are the right fit. No commitment required at this stage.

  • 02
    We issue the partnership agreement

    A one-page digital document confirming the property, the applicable commission rate (0.8% or 1.2%), and the listing duration. Signed electronically, returned within minutes.

  • 03
    We invest — at our own risk

    Production begins immediately. You pay nothing until the property sells. Our team covers all photography, video, 3D, and microsite costs. Our investment is our confidence in what we build.

  • 04
    Settlement on closing

    When the property sells, we issue a single invoice for the agreed commission percentage of the final sale price. Payment is due within 14 days of completion. VAT is applied where applicable under Luxembourg, Italian, or Turkish tax law.

Common Questions

What agents ask
before they commit.

What if the property doesn't sell?

You pay nothing. MERGVS absorbs the full production cost. Our commission only applies when the transaction closes. This is not a risk-sharing arrangement — it is entirely our risk. If the property doesn't sell, we bear the loss.

Are there any costs during production?

No retainer, no deposit, no monthly fee. Production begins after the partnership agreement is signed — all costs are borne by MERGVS until settlement. The only invoice you will ever receive from us arrives 14 days after closing.

Does MERGVS require exclusivity?

No. We do not require exclusivity on the listing. You may continue to market the property through other channels. The MERGVS partnership agreement covers our production and commission terms only — your agency relationship with the seller remains entirely your own.

What geographies do you cover?

MERGVS operates in Luxembourg, Milan, and Istanbul. We also work internationally on a case-by-case basis for properties of sufficient distinction. If your listing falls outside these markets, contact us — we will assess it individually and confirm within 24 hours whether we can engage.

How long does production take?

A standard engagement — 3D environment, photography, video — is typically delivered within 10–15 working days of the site visit. Complex or multi-unit properties may require additional time. We confirm a precise timeline in the partnership agreement, and we commit to it.

How is this different from Matterport or subscription-based 3D tour platforms?

Subscription platforms charge monthly fees regardless of whether the property sells, host the experience on their own domain, and apply the same template to every listing. MERGVS charges nothing until closing, delivers a fully bespoke experience — never templated — and hosts it on a dedicated property URL independent of any platform. No app to download, no vendor account required. Your buyer clicks a link; the world opens.
